Meet the Person Behind the Portfolio

Experienced Regional Director with a demonstrated history of success in the human service industry. As a dedicated professional, I possess a broad range of skills including nonprofit organizations, negotiation, quality management, volunteer management, disability services, employment, residential, clinical & day programs, senior services, education, and workforce development. Additionally, I'm currently enhancing my skill set by developing expertise in FullStack Development. Please feel free to take a look at my resume for further details on my qualifications! I hope you enjoy exploring my page, and I would be excited for the opportunity to collaborate on any projects!


I am an experienced professional in the health and human services field. I have a passion for helping others and have spent the past several years working in various roles within the industry. Most recently, I served as the Director of Day Habilitation Operations at Bridgewell based out of Danvers, MA, where I oversaw their day habilitation funded programs. Prior to this, I worked as the Regional Director at Viability based out of Salem, MA, managing a diverse team across multiple programs and funding sources.

Throughout my career, I have had the opportunity to launch new programs, develop innovative service models, and ensure compliance with regulations and audits. I have also had the pleasure of hiring and developing staff, setting goals and targets, and networking with local committees and collaboratives.

In 2020, amidst the pandemic, I successfully managed a healthcare facility at American Training, and played a vital role in addressing the various challenges that arose during those unprecedented times. I have the ability to adapt to changes, respond to challenges, and create new programs while delivering high-quality services to clients.

My dedication to my work and my commitment to helping those in need has allowed me to make a meaningful impact in the lives of countless individuals and their families. I look forward to continuing to do so in my future endeavors!
